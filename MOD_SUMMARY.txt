================================================================
MARTH REQUIEM OVERHAUL (MRO) v0.3.1 — Mod Summary
================================================================
A terminal load-order mod for Requiem (built against LoreRim) that
rebalances late-game power scaling. Early and mid game are untouched;
every feature gates on endgame investment. Player and followers ascend;
enemies keep their Requiem lethality.

Requires: SKSE64, SkyUI 5.2+, Custom Skills Framework,
          powerofthree's Papyrus Extender
Load order: MRO.esp dead last.

----------------------------------------------------------------
ELEMENTAL RESISTANCES
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Resistance cap removed (75% -> uncapped, fPlayerMaxResistance 10000).
  Fire/frost/shock/magic/poison all scale to 100% and beyond.
  100% = full immunity to that element.
  NOTE: global setting — highly resistant enemies also gain true
  immunity to their own element. Carry a second damage type.

- ELEMENTAL ABSORB (player + followers only):
  Resistance above 100% converts incoming damage of that type to
  healing: 1% of magnitude per point over 100, full 100% heal at 200%.
  Covers hostile spells, weapon enchantments, drains (Magic Resist),
  and poisons — each magic effect is matched to the resistance that
  actually governs it. Only health-damaging effects count (frost slow
  riders etc. are ignored). Toggleable in MCM.

----------------------------------------------------------------
PHYSICAL DAMAGE REDUCTION
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Engine armor cap stays 75% (750 armor rating at 0.10/point).
  The curve below 750 armor is IDENTICAL to the unmodded load order.
- Above 750 armor, player and followers keep scaling via hidden perks:
      DR% = 75 + (armor - 750) x 0.0192
      ~2000 armor rating = 99% DR (near-invulnerable)
  Enemies never benefit; they stay hard-capped at 75%.
  The armor UI still displays 75% — the extra reduction is real but
  not shown in menus. Re-evaluated every 30 seconds. Toggleable in MCM.

----------------------------------------------------------------
SKILL MASTERY SYSTEM (13 skills, via Custom Skills Framework)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Each mastery unlocks when its base skill reaches 100 and advances
  through continued use of that skill. Cap adjustable 50-200 in MCM
  (default 100); XP per level roughly doubles the vanilla 99->100
  effort and grows with depth.
- Bonuses at full mastery:
    One-Handed / Two-Handed / Archery ... +50% attack damage
    Evasion / Heavy Armor ............... +300 armor rating
        (applies ONLY while wearing a chest piece of the matching
         type; this is the intended path to 99% physical DR)
    Destruction / Restoration / Alteration /
    Conjuration / Illusion .............. +50 effective school skill
    Smithing / Alchemy / Enchanting ..... +25% crafting potency
- Toggleable in MCM; live progress shown on the Mastery page.

----------------------------------------------------------------
ECONOMY AND QUALITY OF LIFE
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Vendor gold doubled: all 13 vendor gold pools (general, blacksmiths,
  apothecary, inn, spells, street, five fence tiers), built by doubling
  the winning values from the load order. Baked into the ESP.
- Carry weight +150 (player + followers, toggleable).
- Arrow recovery 66% (vanilla 33%, toggleable).
- Cell reset 72h / 168h cleared (Requiem default 30 days) — shops
  restock reliably; dungeons also repopulate faster (toggleable).

----------------------------------------------------------------
MCM (3 pages)
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Boss Readiness: READY/CAUTION/NOT READY assessment for Alduin
  (both fights), Harkon, Miraak — level, HP pool, fire resist, and
  survivable Drain Vitality pulses. Thresholds auto-raise if World
  Eater's Influence / Immersive Miraak Difficulty are detected.
- Mastery: live level and bonus for all 13 skills; cap slider.
- Features: live toggles for every runtime feature above.

----------------------------------------------------------------
OPTIONAL
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Rebalanced Experience.ini (if using the Experience mod): XP weighted
  toward kills, group fights, above-level enemies, dungeon clears, and
  quest milestones; less from map discovery. Same total speed as the
  Faster Leveling preset.

----------------------------------------------------------------
DESIGN NOTES / KNOWN CAVEATS
----------------------------------------------------------------
- Everything dynamic runs on a 30-second script heartbeat: gear swaps
  can take up to 30s to re-tier DR and mastery bonuses.
- Absorb reads base enchantment records for weapons (NPC gear exact;
  player-crafted enchant strength approximated).
- Physical DR perks cover weapon/creature hits, not traps or falls.
- Both vendor gold and the DR curve were derived from the live load
  order; regenerate MRO.esp after major list updates.
